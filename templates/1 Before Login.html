Steps for applying online:

1. Please read the detailed rolling advertisement before you start filling out the Application Form. Click Here for Advertisement.

2. Please read the detailed faculty recruitment advertisement advertisement before you start filling out the Application Form. Click Here for Advertisement.

Steps to be followed to apply online

2. Create/Register Your Account:-

i) Create your account by clicking on Create new account Link in the left User login Section.

ii) Enter Your Email address and username and click on create new account button.

iii) Login detail will send on your registered email address.

iv) One candidate has to register once only for all the posts.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. Login and Complete the application form:-

i) After Login click on the Rolling Adv. 06/2023 tab on the Menu bar and Click on the corresponding department link to apply in that department.

ii) Complete the Application Form:  Enter the complete details in all sections of the application form and then click on Final Submission.

iii) Confirmation Email: You will get an email in your registered email address after the successful submission of the application form.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. Instructions to save the draft of the Application:-

i) In the application form there is a Save Draft button that only saves the detail in draft mode.

ii) For successful submission of your application form, you have to fill in all detail and then click on the Final Submit button.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. Instructions to Edit the application form:-

i) Candidates can submit only one application form for each post.

ii) If a candidate wants to make any change to the submitted application form he/she has to go to the edit section and make the desired changes and again click on the Final Submission button.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. Instructions to upload files/documents/PDF/image:- 

i) Rename your pdf/documents/image as yourname_some_unique_number before uploading them on portal. Please do not use special character except then underscore (_)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. Candidate have to apply ‘ON-LINE’ only through the official website.

8. One candidate has to register once only for all the posts.

9. Candidates must apply separately for different departments.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

10. Help Desk/Contact Us:

i) For any type of queries, contact us through  only email address:- line211
ii) No other means of contact should be entertained.

11. Reset/Forgot password:-

i) More than 3 attempts of the wrong password will block your account.

ii) If you forgot your password please reset it by using Reset your password link.

iii) Password reset detail will be sent to your registered email address.
